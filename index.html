About

I am especially interested in the intersection between industrial automation, embedded systems, and practical field engineering. I enjoy understanding how machines behave, why failures happen, and how controls can be improved without unnecessary cost.

My strongest engineering instinct is troubleshooting: tracing faults logically, understanding signal behavior, and designing solutions that are practical, maintainable, and reliable.

How I Think as an Engineer

Practical Engineering
